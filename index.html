Project Jo-Jo

// Active Build
JO-JO
1964 VW Beetle — Mid-Engine Restomod

A ground-up chassis rebuild transforming a classic air-cooled Beetle into a purpose-built, mid-engine performance machine. Every component is engineered from scratch — from the custom tubular chassis and bespoke multi-link suspension to the EJ25 Subaru flat-four repositioned and modified to live behind the driver. This is not a kit build. This is engineering from first principles, documented in full.

Services

Chassis Fabrication

Custom tubular and sheet-metal chassis design and fabrication. From ground-up builds to structural modifications — engineered to spec, built to last.

Aero Component Design

Bespoke aerodynamic components for OEM+ and full custom applications. CFD-informed design through to manufactured parts — splitters, diffusers, wings, and bodywork.

Suspension Engineering

Purpose-built suspension geometry design. Multi-link, double wishbone, bespoke uprights — engineered for the application, not adapted from a catalogue.

Powertrain Adaptation

Engine swaps, custom mounts, drivetrain packaging, and bespoke engine modification for non-standard installations — including mid and rear-engine conversions.

OEM+ Manufacture

Small-batch production of performance components for road and track. Precision fabrication with attention to fitment, finish, and function.

Design Consultation

Engineering consultation for builders, fabricators, and manufacturers. Concept-to-spec support for projects that need a technical co-pilot from the start.
